Clickbait

Summary

Clickbait is a short thriller series on Netflix. In the beginning of the season, Nick is suddenly kidnapped and his sister and wife begin a search to find him. While Nick’s character is a great father and husband, the person who has kidnapped him accuses him of abusing women. It turns out, that someone in Nick’s life was using his picture on an online dating app and connecting with women–and one connection leads to a very tragic situation. The show emphasizes how you can’t believe everything you see or everyone you meet online.

Review

I thought that Clickbait was overall a very action-packed and engaging series. I watched the show very quickly and was always curious as to what was going to happen next. I also think that in such a digital age, the show shed light on a very important topic in an interesting way.
